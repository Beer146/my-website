Welcome to My DevOps Journey

Hi! I’m Abhir Naik, a Computer Science and Business Administration student at Northeastern University, concentrating in FinTech. I’m passionate about the intersection of software engineering, automation, and cloud infrastructure—essentially, everything that makes modern DevOps so exciting.

Beyond professional experience, I’ve built my own home lab to simulate real-world cloud environments. It’s where I experiment with containerized applications, monitoring, and continuous deployment workflows. My goal is to continue learning how to design robust cloud infrastructures and help teams build automated, secure, and scalable systems.
